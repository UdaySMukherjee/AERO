Components Used

BMP180

The BMP180 is a barometric pressure and temperature sensor, commonly used in weather stations and altimeters, providing accurate measurements for atmospheric pressure and temperature.

DHT11

The DHT11 is a digital temperature and humidity sensor, capable of measuring ambient temperature and relative humidity, making it ideal for home automation and environmental monitoring applications.

MQ-Gas Sensors

MQ gas sensors are widely used for detecting various gases in the environment, providing valuable insights for safety and monitoring applications. Their high sensitivity and quick response make them essential tools in gas detection systems.
